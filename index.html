About My Rights

Empowering citizens with knowledge about their rights and entitlements

🎯

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between government welfare schemes and eligible citizens by providing an easy-to-use platform that helps people discover benefits they are entitled to.

🤝

Who We Help

Students, farmers, women, senior citizens, differently abled persons, and all citizens of India — especially Tamil Nadu — looking for government assistance.

⚡

How It Works

Our intelligent eligibility checker matches your profile against 55+ government schemes and instantly shows you only those schemes you qualify for.
